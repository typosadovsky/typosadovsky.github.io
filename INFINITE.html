MY STORY

Discovered Passion for Technology

At a very young age, I discovered my passion for technology. All through elementary and middle school, I loved to make record and edit videos for all my school projects. I even started doing simple animations from the age of 13. I knew that I wanted to go into something to do with multimedia.

Won Communications Technology Award

My favourite class throughout highschool was Communications Technology. In this class, we made movies, and made many graphic design projects using the Adobe Suite. I excelled in this class in grade 12 and won an award for having the highest mark in the class.

Accepted Into Carleton University

I discovered the Interactive Multimedia and Design program at Carleton in a brochure, and instantly knew it was the perfect program for me. It is a very prestigious program, with only 60 new accepted students every year. I had to submit a portfolio of my work, and after months of waiting, I recieved the email that I had been accepted to the program.

Enhanced Skills and Learned New Ones

Throughout my studies in this program, I greatly improved on my skills that I already had, such as Photoshop and movie making. However, I also gained many new skills, such as programming, web development and 3D animation. My skills are continuing to grow every day.

Got on the Dean's Honour List

After my first year at Carleton, I had such high academic success that I made it onto the Dean's Honour List. This was a great achievement for me because it reassured me that I am definately in the right program and I am going down the right path.

Gaining Co-op Experience

When I chose this program at Carleton, I also chose the Co-op option. This really appealed to me because gaining real work experience during your studies can be very beneficial in the long run. I am hoping to use this experience to my advantage and take a lot away from it.
